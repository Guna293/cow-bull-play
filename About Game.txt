               COW-BULL GAME
            --------------------
-->In this game you will have 8 chances to guess the number correctly.
BULL--> place value of a number is matched.
COW-->value present in the number.
ex:456 and 485
ans: 1 bull(for 4),1 cow (for 5)
ex2:986 and 829
ans:0 bull ,2 cow(for 9,8)
